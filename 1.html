Ganesh Utsav:

Ganesh Chaturthi, also known as Omkar Utsav, is a festival celebrating the arrival of Lord Ganesha to earth from Kailash Parvat with his mother Goddess Parvati. Though there is not much known about how the celebration of this occasion began, the festival has been collectively celebrated in Pune since the era of Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The Ganesha festival lost state patronage and became a private family celebration in Maharashtra after the onset of the British Raj. However, it was revived by Indian freedom fighter and social reformer Lokmanya Tilak and is celebrated with ebullience and rejoicing till date.
